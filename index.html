Separation of Concerns

This is what I mean by 'focusing one's attention upon some aspect': it does not mean ignoring the other aspects, it is just doing justice to the fact that from this aspect's point of view, the other is irrelevant. It is being one- and multiple-track minded simultaneously.

- by Edsger W. Dijkstra in his 1974 paper "On the Role of Scientific Thought"

Responsibility-driven design

Abstraction

Separating the behavior of software components from their implementation

Latin root is "abstractus" which means “to draw away”.
In other words, to hide!

Failure to separate behavior from implementation is a common cause of unnecessary coupling.

Our profession is an abstraction on top of an abstraction on top of an abstraction...

  • Data hiding
  • Control abstraction
  • Classes/Interfaces
  • Higher-order Components

Anticipate of Change

Software purpose is to automate a solution to a problem.

- Developers are learning the domain from their client - The clients are learning the extent that can be automated - When those collide, there is change.

Low Coupling

Degree of interdependence between software components. Inter-module binding.

High Cohesion

Components that are easier to use when requirements change. Intra-module binding.

Generality

Designing software that is free from unnatural restrictions and limitations

Consistency
